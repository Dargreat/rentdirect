Safety Tips for Tenants

  • Always confirm the landlord's identity before making any form of payment.
  • Keep all communications within the in-app messaging system until you're fully confident.
  • Never pay the full rent before physically viewing the property or confirming its ownership.
  • Use the app's Report feature to flag any suspicious listings or messages immediately.
  • Either visit the property yourself or send a trusted representative before making any commitments.
