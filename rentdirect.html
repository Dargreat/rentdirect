RentDirect Listing Guidelines & Pricing

List Your Property with Ease

At RentDirect, we connect verified landlords directly with tenants — no middlemen, no fraud. To maintain a safe and reliable platform for everyone, here’s how our property listing works:

Who Can List?

  • Verified property owners
  • Authorized property managers (with proof)

How to List

Option 1: Free Listing

List your property for FREE on our website by completing a simple verification:

  • Upload a valid Ghana Card, Passport, or Driver’s License
  • Upload clear property photos and description
  • Provide a contact for tenants to reach out to you
  • Once approved, your listing goes live immediately

Option 2: WhatsApp Listing Support (GHS 50)

Let our team list for you via WhatsApp:

  • Send your property photos, videos, description, rent amount, location details, and your Mobile Money number to 0531128932
  • Pay a GHS 50 listing support fee
  • Our team will handle the listing for you

Every landlord must complete a one-time verification to protect tenants and prevent fraud. Listings from unverified landlords will not be approved.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Rent Direct

How do I pay for the service?
We accept mobile money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo), credit/debit cards, and bank transfers. All payments are securely processed.
Is property listing really free for landlords?
Yes! Landlords can list properties completely free by completing our verification process. We only charge a fee if you want our support team to handle the listing for you via WhatsApp.
What happens after I pay to unlock a contact?
Immediately after payment, you'll receive the landlord's direct contact information (phone number and/or email) and can reach out to them directly to arrange viewings and discuss the rental.
How long does verification take for landlords?
Our verification process typically takes 24-48 hours. Once approved, your listing will go live immediately.
